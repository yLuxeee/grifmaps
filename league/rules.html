League Rules

  1. Be respectful to players and staff.
  2. Matches are best-of-3 unless playoffs specify otherwise.
  3. No intentional griefing, cheating, or stat padding.
  4. Captains must report match results within 24 hours.
  5. Schedule conflicts should be communicated early.
